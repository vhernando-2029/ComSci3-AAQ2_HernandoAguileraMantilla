Mental Health Conditions, Information & Symptoms

Anxiety Disorders

a condition where a person feels excessive fear, worry, or nervousness that does not go away easily

Common Signs & Symptoms:

  • Constant worry
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Sweating or shaking
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Overthinking

Depression

a mood disorder that causes deep sadness, hopelessness, and lack of motivation for long periods of time.

Common signs & symptoms:

  • Feeling sad most of the time
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Fatigue
  • Changes in appetite
  • Thoughts of worthlessness

Bipolar Disorder

  • Sudden energy bursts
  • Risky behavior
  • Long periods of sadness
  • Difficulty sleeping

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

a condition that develops after experiencing or seeing a traumatic event.

Common signs & symptoms:

  • Flashbacks
  • Nightmares
  • Fear and panic
  • Avoiding reminders of trauma

Eating Disorders

these are serious conditions related to unhealthy eating behaviors and distorted body image.

Examples:

  • Anorexia
  • Bulimia
  • Binge-eating disorder

ADHD (Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder)

a condition that affects attention, focus, and self-control.

Common signs & symptoms:

  • Difficulty focusing
  • Forgetfulness
  • Restlessness
  • Impulsiveness

Schizophrenia

a severe mental disorder that affects a person's thinking, perception, and behavior.
